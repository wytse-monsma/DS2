BAHIA COCOA REVIEW Showers continued throughout the week in the Bahia cocoa zone , alleviating the drought since early January and improving prospect for the coming temporao , although normal humidity level have not been restored , Comissaria Smith said in it weekly review . The dry period mean the temporao will be late this year . Arrivals for the week ended February 22 were 155,221 bag of 60 kilo making a cumulative total for the season of 5.93 mln against 5.81 at the same stage last year . Again it seems that cocoa delivered earlier on consignment wa included in the arrival figure . Comissaria Smith said there is still some doubt a to how much old crop cocoa is still available a harvesting ha practically come to an end . With total Bahia crop estimate around 6.4 mln bag and sale standing at almost 6.2 mln there are a few hundred thousand bag still in the hand of farmer , middleman , exporter and processor . There are doubt a to how much of this cocoa would be fit for export a shipper are now experiencing dificulties in obtaining +Bahia superior+ certificate . In view of the lower quality over recent week farmer have sold a good part of their cocoa held on consignment . Comissaria Smith said spot bean price rose to 340 to 350 cruzados per arroba of 15 kilo . Bean shipper were reluctant to offer nearby shipment and only limited sale were booked for March shipment at 1,750 to 1,780 dlrs per tonne to port to be named . New crop sale were also light and all to open port with June/July going at 1,850 and 1,880 dlrs and at 35 and 45 dlrs under New York july , Aug/Sept at 1,870 , 1,875 and 1,880 dlrs per tonne FOB . Routine sale of butter were made . March/April sold at 4,340 , 4,345 and 4,350 dlrs . April/May butter went at 2.27 time New York May , June/July at 4,400 and 4,415 dlrs , Aug/Sept at 4,351 to 4,450 dlrs and at 2.27 and 2.28 time New York Sept and Oct/Dec at 4,480 dlrs and 2.27 time New York Dec , Comissaria Smith said . Destinations were the U.S. , Covertible currency area , Uruguay and open port . Cake sale were registered at 785 to 995 dlrs for March/April , 785 dlrs for May , 753 dlrs for Aug and 0.39 time New York Dec for Oct/Dec . Buyers were the U.S. , Argentina , Uruguay and convertible currency area . Liquor sale were limited with March/April selling at 2,325 and 2,380 dlrs , June/July at 2,375 dlrs and at 1.25 time New York July , Aug/Sept at 2,400 dlrs and at 1.25 time New York Sept and Oct/Dec at 1.25 time New York Dec , Comissaria Smith said . Total Bahia sale are currently estimated at 6.13 mln bag against the 1986/87 crop and 1.06 mln bag against the 1987/88 crop . Final figure for the period to February 28 are expected to be published by the Brazilian Cocoa Trade Commission after carnival which end midday on February 27 .
